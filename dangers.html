The Damage

What are the signs that someone has a drug problem?
1. Changing friends a lot
2. Spending a lot of time alone
3. Losing interest in favorite things
4. Not taking care of themselves - for example: not taking showers, changing clothes, or brushing their teeth
5. Being really tired and sad
6. Eating more or eating less than usual
7. Being very energetic, talking fast, or saying things that don't make sense
8. Sleeping at strange hours
9. Missing important appointments
10. Having problems in personal or family relationships
Risks of drug use:
1. Developing a tolerance to the drug
2. Withdrawal symptoms when trying to cut back or quit
3. Health problems, like irregular heart rate, high blood pressure, lung damage, and seizures
4. Injury, both accidental and self-inflicted
5. Overdose
6. Death
